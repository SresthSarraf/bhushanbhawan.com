Heritage & Legacy

Crafting Beauty Since 1925

Nestled in the heart of Patna's historic Bakarganj, Bhushan Bhawan Jewellers has been a beacon of fine craftsmanship for nearly a century. Our legacy is woven into every piece we create.

From intricate gold filigree work inspired by ancient Bihar traditions to contemporary diamond settings, each creation tells a story of our artisans' devotion to their craft — passed down through generations.

Leadership

Shashi Kumar

Director, Bhushan Bhawan Jewellers

Under the leadership of Shri Shashi Kumar, Bhushan Bhawan has grown from a trusted local jeweller into one of Patna's most celebrated heritage jewellery destinations — honouring the legacy of the late Shri Umesh Kumar who laid the foundation in 1925.

With a commitment to preserving the finest traditions of Indian jewellery craftsmanship while embracing the designs of a new era, our team continues to create masterpieces that are treasured across generations.

We assure every customer the very best in quality, purity, and satisfaction — values that have been at the heart of Bhushan Bhawan for a century.

Our Vision

A Leading Creative Jewellery Destination

Bhushan Bhawan aspires to be the most preferred and revered jewellery brand in Patna and subsequently across India — making exclusive, top-end global and heritage designs accessible to all, providing complete customer delight.

Our ability to cut and polish gemstones gives us an advantage to deliver quality products in a timely manner, building long-lasting relationships with every customer.

✦
Our Quality

BIS Hallmarked. Purity Guaranteed.

All our gold jewellery is BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) certified and hallmarked, ensuring every piece you purchase meets the highest purity standards set by the Government of India.

From the sourcing of raw materials to the final polish, every step is guided by our century-old philosophy: quality, purity, and the complete satisfaction of our valued customers.
